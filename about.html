About Eastern Travel Mart

The Eastern Travel Mart (ETM) 2026 is one of the leading tourism trade platforms in Eastern Nepal designed to connect tourism stakeholders with national and international markets.

The mart brings together tour operators, travel agencies, destination managers, hospitality brands, airlines, tourism boards, and content creators to explore new opportunities, strengthen partnerships, and promote tourism destinations.

Tourism Sellers & Exhibitors
